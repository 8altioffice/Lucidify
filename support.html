Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings -> Clear All Data.

2. How do I restore my purchases?

If you have switched devices or reinstalled the app, you can restore your premium subscription by going to the premium Upgrade screen and tapping Restore Purchases.

3. Does the app need an internet connection?

An internet connection is required to use AI features (dream interpretation and AI image generation) and Cloud storage syncing (if configured by OS). Basic journaling and offline features do not require an active internet connection.

4. How is my privacy protected?

We do not store your data on our servers. Your data stays securely on your device. AI features use external secure APIs.
